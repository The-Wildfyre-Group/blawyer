  • Bio

    Xavier Capital Management, LLC is a registered investment adviser specializing in fixed income portfolios. The firm, headquartered in Largo, Maryland, was founded in 2006 with the goal of delivering attractive risk-adjusted returns to client portfolios by integrating the highest industry standards on a smaller, more flexible platform.

    Xavier Capital is dedicated to providing clients with the highest level of service and building strong, value-added relationships over time. We market our products and services to various types of institutional clients including public funds, corporations, endowments and foundations, hospitals, religious organizations and Taft-Hartley plans. Xavier Capital is 100% employee owned.
